About Campus Connect

Campus Connect is a cutting-edge RFID-based system designed to revolutionize university operations. Our system integrates attendance tracking and cashless transactions, providing a seamless and efficient experience for students, faculty, and administrators. We are committed to enhancing campus life through innovative technology and user-centric design.

Our Features

Empowering Your Campus

Campus Connect offers a suite of features designed to streamline campus operations and enhance the student experience.

Smart Attendance

Automated attendance tracking using RFID technology, ensuring accurate and efficient record-keeping.

Digital Wallet

Secure and convenient cashless transactions for campus services, eliminating the need for physical cash.

Real-Time Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard providing real-time insights into attendance, transactions, and system usage.

Parent-Student Transparency

Enhanced communication and transparency between parents and students regarding attendance and financial activities.
